CRYSTALGRICHE

Crystalgriche (\kʁis.tal.ɡʁiʃ\) is Stephanie Castonguay and Sylvain Aubé. The project is based on the utilisation of small handmade electronic objects, made by Stephanie Castonguay, together with pedals, analog and modular synths.

On the invitation of the Sight+Sound Festival, the duo creates a 30 minutes improvisation cycle called Terra Mater. In a raw aesthetic, inspired among others by Martin Howse, the duo explores physical elements through rocks, crystals and sediments, used for their magnetic and electric conductivity.

The tracks on "Terra Mater" are made of several iterations of this improvisation cycle, combining studio and live versions.

"Terra Mater" is available on tape, or in the digital format, at griche.bandcamp.com/
